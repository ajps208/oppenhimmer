ABOUT

Oppenheimer, a gripping IMAX®-shot epic thriller directed by Christopher Nolan, delves into the enigmatic life of J. Robert Oppenheimer, brilliantly portrayed by Cillian Murphy. Set during the Manhattan Project, Oppenheimer faces the heart-wrenching dilemma of risking the world's destruction to save it. Alongside Emily Blunt as his wife, Matt Damon as General Leslie Groves Jr., and Robert Downey Jr. as Lewis Strauss, the film explores the intense dynamics of this historic event. With a stellar cast, including Rami Malek and Kenneth Branagh, Nolan once again showcases his filmmaking prowess. Based on the Pulitzer Prize-winning book, Oppenheimer promises a visually stunning and emotionally charged experience, blending IMAX® black and white analogue photography with 65mm large-format film.
